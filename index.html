About Us

SHASHI SHIELD FORTIFICATION PVT. LTD. came to life two decades ago. The zeal to strive for better and make a mark among the masses was the spark that ignited the idea of Multi-Facility Services. Mr. Ganesh G., CEO, and Mr. Shashi Kumar, MD, felt the pulse of both individuals and business owners concerning the challenge of maintaining cleanliness on a daily basis—especially in the current scenario, where there is a shortage of experienced, trained, and trusted personnel. At the same time, security became a pressing issue with the rise in thefts everywhere. On the other hand, both observed that eligible youth were idling away their time due to a lack of jobs. This led to the phenomenal idea of bringing Multi-Facility Services under one roof. Both partners have worked relentlessly to bring SHASHI SHIELD FORTIFICATION PVT. LTD. to a level where their services are now seen as the need of the hour. SHASHI SHIELD FORTIFICATION PVT. LTD. has carved a niche in the ever-growing and demanding industry of Security & Facility Management Services. It is a customer-friendly company that caters to the diverse needs of both individual and commercial clients. We help our customers focus on their core business and expand their horizons, thus contributing to our nation’s economy and bringing it a step closer to becoming a superpower.


VISION
